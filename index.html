Application of Procedures and Regulations

Both crewmembers will identify and apply appropriate
procedures in accordance with published operating instructions
and applicable regulations

Flight Path Managment

The PF will control the flight path manually or through the use of
automation, as appropriate. The PM will monitor the flight path parameters
and will promptly announce any deviations.

Situation Awareness

Both crewmembers will perceive, comprehend, and manage information and
anticipate its affect on the operation.

Problem Solving and Decision-Making

Both crewmembers will identyfy and resolve problems using appropriate
decision-making methodology.

Workload and Time Management

Both crewmembers will manage available resources efficiently
by prioritizing and performing tasks in a timely manner.

Teamwork and Leadership

Both crewmembers will collaborate to accomplish the operational
goals and will influence others to contribute to a shared purpose.

Communications

Both crewmembers will demonstrate appropriate and effective oral, non-verbal,
and written communications, in normal and non-normal situations.
